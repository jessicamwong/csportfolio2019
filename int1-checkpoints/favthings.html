Favorite Things

Favorite Thing #1: Fruits

Fruits are very healthy and help humans maintain a balanced diet. One way to identify if a food is a fruit is to look if there are seeds in the inside of the food. Fruits contain lots of nutritous vitamins and healthy sugars.

Favorite Thing #2: Scenery

One way to appreciate the things in life is to go sightseeing and enjoy all the beautiful scenery. Starring at scenery and listening to the birds chirping or the waves crashing can often be very peaceful and relaxing. This allows you to see the joys of nature!
